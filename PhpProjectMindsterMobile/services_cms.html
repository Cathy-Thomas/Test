What is CMS

Content Management System is an easy way to manage the content of a website. It is one of those sources through which even a layman can upgrade, edit and change the content on his website. CMSs are known for the ease that they provide to the owners of the websites and the features they are capable of providing. They help you keeping the content fresh and upgraded, making it more appealing to your users. CMS also makes a website more search-engine friendly and thus, you can rank higher on the search engines.

CMS are basically a result of two sub components namely Content Management Application (CMA) and Content Delivery Application (CDA). CMA is responsible for the management of the content on the website whereas CDA takes care of compiling the information that updates the content.

Other benefits of a CMS are

  • A CMS web site is database driven
  • Separate design and content
  • Uses cascading style sheet (CSS) to control site appearance
  • Multiple authors
  • Access from anywhere
  • Web site management panel
  • Schedule content updates
  • HTML knowledge not required
  • Saves time
  • Creates search-engine friendly pages
  • Attracts visitors
  • Create automatic RSS Feeds
