What is Drupal?

Drupal is a PHP/MySQL based open source content management system. Known for its highly useful and standard features, Drupal helps in uploading content of websites with the input of minimal effort.

Why Drupal?

What makes Drupal ideal for e-commerce website development is its standard structure and the functionalities it offers. All the functionalities of Drupal are available in add-on modules and core layers. Moreover, its features can be plugged-in as per the requirement.
